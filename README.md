## Overview

Welcome to my Pokémon-like game! This game is inspired by the classic Pokémon series but offers a simpler experience. Players can explore a single map and engage in battles with wild monsters. Perfect for fans of monster-catching games who want a quick and nostalgic experience.


## Features

- Explore a single, unique map filled with wild monsters.
- Engage in battles with various wild monsters.
- Simple and intuitive controls.

## How to Play

- **Start the Game:** Launch the game executable.
- **Explore the Map:** Use the arrow keys to move your character around the map.
- **Encounter Wild Monsters:** Walk into tall grass or designated areas to trigger wild monster encounters.
- **Battle:** Engage in battles with the wild monsters using your team of monsters.

## Controls
- **Arrow Keys:** Move your character.
- **Left-click:** Confirm selections and interact with objects.

## Battle System

- **Engaging Battles:** When you encounter a wild monster, the battle screen will appear.
- **Turn-based Combat:** Select from available moves to attack the wild monster. Each monster has its own set of moves and abilities.
- **Winning Battles:** Reduce the wild monster’s HP to zero to win the battle.
- **Losing Battles:** If your monster’s HP is reduced to zero, you will lose the battle and return to the starting point.

## Map
- **Single Map:** The game features one carefully designed map filled with various terrains and wild monster habitats.
- **Exploration:** Move around to find different types of wild monsters. Each area of the map may feature unique monsters.

## Known Issues

Limited Content: The game currently only has one map and limited variations of wild monsters. More content and features are planned for future updates.

## Conclusion

Thank you for exploring my Pokémon-style JavaScript game! I hope you enjoyed playing and experiencing the nostalgia of classic Pokémon adventures.
